THE PROBLEM
1M+ slip-and-fall injuries per year on icy sidewalks
$35B municipal liability and clearing costs

Cities are legally required to clear sidewalks but lack the labor. Manual crews are expensive, unreliable, and can't respond fast enough. Property owners ignore ordinances. People get hurt. Cities get sued.

THE SOLUTION

Teleoperated rovers clear sidewalks with one operator monitoring a fleet of ten. That's 10x cost reduction over manual labor.

Sidewalk-scale robots (not trucks). Modular tools for year-round use: snow clearing in winter, mowing in summer.

Geometry-based safety (no ML in the critical path). Open source hardware and software.
