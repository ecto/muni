We watched people (parents with strollers, elderly neighbors,
kids walking to school) forced into the street because there
was no safe path on the sidewalk. Sidewalks are public
infrastructure. They deserve the same attention we give to roads.

So we asked a simple question: why are we still clearing sidewalks
by hand? Why isn't there a better way? We're building that future
in the open, one rover at a time.

muni was founded in 2025 in Cleveland, Ohio. We're a small team
of engineers who believe public spaces should work for everyone.
